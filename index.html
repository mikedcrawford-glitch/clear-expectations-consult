About Debbie

Debbie isn’t a doctor — and that’s the point. She has lived experience with cosmetic body procedures and learned firsthand that recovery can be harder than expected, emotions can shift, and results don’t always match the sales pitch.

This is a calm, honest space to talk through expectations, recovery experiences, and emotional challenges with someone who truly understands.

Who This Is For

This service is for people considering cosmetic surgery or those who are post-surgery and struggling emotionally. It is not medical advice, not therapy, and not a replacement for professional care.

What to Expect

Sessions are private, confidential, and conversation-based. There is no pressure, no sales pitch, and no promises — just honest discussion and support.

Clear Expectations Consult provides peer-based, experience-driven support only. No medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment is provided.
